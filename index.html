Safety and Rules

  • Always follow the instructions of the campsite staff and guides.
  • Swim only in designated swimming areas and at your own risk.
  • Keep a safe distance from wildlife and avoid feeding them.
  • Carry and use mosquito repellent to avoid insect bites.
  • Do not litter. Keep the camping site and the lake clean.
  • Respect the privacy and tranquility of other campers.
  • Do not make excessive noise, especially during quiet hours.
  • Stay hydrated and carry enough water during outdoor activities.
  • Do not venture into restricted or off-limits areas.
  • Be mindful of campfires.

  How to reach the Indraprastha- Dream World ?
You can reach Indraprastha- Dream World by private vehicle. There is a parking space for each campsite. Reach Daund via train or bus if you are coming by public transport. Read more about how to reach Indraprastha- Dream World?
  How are the tent arrangements ?
You will get a separate 2 people, 3 people or 4 people Non-AC tents according to your choice and availability. You don’t have to share a tent with an unknown person. Foam mattresses, bed-sheet, pillow, and blanket will be provided in the tent. (We don’t provide sleeping bags for hygiene purpose)
  What are the food options while camping ?
The food options are as follows:
1. Snacks: tea and pakoda
2. Veg-BBQ: marinated paneer with assorted veggies
3. NonVeg-BBQ: marinated chicken with assorted veggies
4. Veg-dinner: veg or paneer sabzi, chapati, dal-tadka, steamed rice, salad, sweet.
5. Nonveg-dinner: chicken rassa, egg curry, egg masala , chapati, steamed rice, salad, sweet.
6. Breakfast: Tea Poha, Bread, Jam.

  What is the cancellation policy ?
Cancellation policy is as follows:
A) 33% reservation-advance is no-refundable in case of total cancellation. However, camping can be rescheduled up to the next year if cancellation is made 24 hours before check-in time.
B) 33% reservation-advance is adjustable (In the final payment) in case of partial cancellation if partial cancellation is made 24 hours before check-in time, Else guest has to pay the full amount.
C) If the guest has fully paid for the booking and cancellations are made 24 hours before the check-in time, then 33% of the total amount will be charged as cancellation fees and the remaining 67% amount will be refunded within 5 working days. If the guest fails to cancel the booking before 24 Hrs then the guest has to pay the full amount.
D) If the guest has fully paid for the booking and partial cancellation is made 24 hours before the check-in time, then an adjustment will be made in the final payment Else no refund will be provided.
